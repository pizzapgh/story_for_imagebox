My Story

To

My name is Chris Brennan, and I'm a graphic designer & self-taught front-end developer. When I turned 18 I moved to Pittsburgh where I got my Bachelors degree in Graphic Design from The Art Institute of Pittsburgh. Before college, I grew up in the southwest, northwest and midwest regions of the country. I'm fortunate to say I can name multiple states as my home, but Phoenix, Arizona is where it all started for me.

Self-Taught

While working as a graphic artist out of college, I began to dabble in HTML/CSS and was overwhelmed with the sea of learning resources available on the web. Dabbling soon became an obsession; instead of going out on weekends, I stayed inside following tutorials and creating web components.

Through tutorials, side projects, constant repetition, grit and hunger for knowledge, I transformed myself into a designer/developer hybrid who can utilize his skills to solve complicated yet fun problems.

Typical Day

A typical day starts out with me waking up in the morning to design and code websites and then off to work from 12-6; serving fresh produce to the locals. After work, I come home to design and code, and do it all over again the next day.

This year I learned about Jekyll development. For the new year, I look forward moving away from Jekyll and expanding my knowledge on WordPress theme development, php, and javascript. In the fast ever-changing world of technology and design, the desire to keep learning and growing is what continues to inspire me. I would be a great fit to learn alongside Imagebox's team of designers and developers. In return, I'll bring my keen eye for design, creative problem solving skills and css expertise to the table.
